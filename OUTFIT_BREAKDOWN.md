## üí° Pro Tips

1. **Pack light, pack smart:** You'll repeat swimwear and casual outfits
2. **Quick-dry is key:** Especially for Day 4 adventure day
3. **Layers:** Mornings and evenings can be cooler, especially on the boat
4. **Sun protection:** Costa Rica sun is strong - hats, sunglasses, sunscreen essential
5. **Comfortable shoes:** You'll be walking, exploring, and on your feet a lot
6. **Waterproof bag:** Essential for Day 4 adventure and Day 5 catamaran
7. **Private Chef Dinner:** This is your nicest dinner - pack something you feel good in!
